⚙️ Complete Smart Road System

The Smart Road System integrates multiple components into one unified system: water collection, storage, distribution, and automation.

Rainwater is collected from the road, stored underground, and reused for cleaning and irrigation through an automated control system.

Sensors monitor conditions such as water level, soil moisture, and dust, while the control unit decides when to activate cleaning or irrigation.

The system is powered by renewable energy sources such as solar panels and road-based dynamo systems, making it sustainable and energy-efficient.
