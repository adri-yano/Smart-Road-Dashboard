🌧️ Drainage & Water Collection

The drainage system collects rainwater through intake grates placed along the road edges. These grates allow water to flow into underground trapezoidal reservoirs.

Inside these reservoirs, heavy particles and dirt settle at the bottom, acting as a natural filtration system before the water moves to the main storage tank.

This stage ensures that only relatively clean water is stored and reused, improving efficiency and reducing maintenance.
